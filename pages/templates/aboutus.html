What is Debatr?

Debatr© is an innovative, open-source platform which allows people from around the globe to engage in informed and respectful discourse about the most pressing issues of our time. Additionally, we serve the community by providing impartial and informative articles, videos and other resources on pressing topics, curated by staff and users.

From social issues to economics, ethics and metaphysics, Debatr© aims to drive humanity away from our conformative bias tendencies towards real logical dialogue and, thus, hopefully, closer to real answers!

Services

We offer two mediums by which users can engage in discourse

1) A simple text-based messaging debate client in where two users can discuss a selected issue via instant message

2) A video client, in where two or more users engage live, face-to-face at an agreed upon time for an agreed upon period

We also hope to implement user pages, which will allow users to follow one another, share past debates, stream live debates and more

In addition, we hope to provide our community with informative articles, videos and other resources under our "For Your Consideration" tab to help drive the conversation.

Rules

We are very well aware of the potential for Debatr© to fall into an abyss of logical fallacy and childish argument, which is far from what we want! The goal of Debatr© is to foster a safe place to question our presumed assumptions about the world. However, this does not mean we are tolerant of racism, xenophobia and pseudo-science.

Thus, we are in the works of implementing various AI solutions to ensure debates are not ridden with bickering, un-cited claims, ad hominems and other common logical fallacies.

The general rules for debates are as follows:

1) No ad hominems (Repeated use of ad hominems will result in account suspension!)

2) Making a scientific claim (e.g. from research, statistics, etc.)? Cite it!

3) Watch your other logical fallacies (we offer you a fallacy reference in both debate platforms)

4) Dont bicker!
